In our highly technological society, where we spend a lot of time looking at computer screens, I would like to shift eyes focus on real things, such as cityscapes, portraits of people, flowers and many other everyday things. It is not just painting it is a short story, a scene with actors, decorations and lights.
It's a vision: to make everyday things unique and scarce.
Often I use Realism as my technique, look from a different angle and use different lighting techniques, glows and shadows.
